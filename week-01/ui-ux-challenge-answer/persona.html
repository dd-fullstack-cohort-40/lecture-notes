Julian (intermediate snowboarder)

Tech

Julian has an iphone 14 pro max and no computer.

Proficiency

Julian is mobile tech savvy, regularly recording snowboarding videos, editing them and posting them on social media. He snowboards with his friends and includes them in his videos.

Attitudes and Needs

Julian quit his cubicle job to work at a ski area and pursue a future as a social media influencer.

Julian doesn't have a computer because he's always on the go. He does everything on his phone, including checking tomorrow's weather to plan riding with his friends.

Julian is trying to improve his boarding skills so he can make the coolest videos and expand his social media reach. One of the ways he tries to improve is by getting the best equipment. He is willing to pay more for a snowboard if he believes that it will help him ride better.

Frustrations

Julian gets frustrated with equipment (snowboard) shopping. He doesn't understand the technical details enough to know if he is getting the right equipment.
