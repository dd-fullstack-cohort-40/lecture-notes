Julian's Use Case and Interaction Flow

Use Case

Julian wants to get a new snowboard that fits his body and personal preferences, but wants expert guidance in the process.

Pre-condition

Julian is on the chairlift with a new snowboarder friend who recommends he checkout CustomCult.com for his next board. Julian immediately takes out his iphone and goes to the website.

Post-condition

Julian gets a recommendation for a custom snowboard design, feels confident enough to buy it and adds it to the shopping cart.

Interaction Flow

  • Julian arrives at customcult.com
  • The website displays a modal with instructions and a start button.
    • Enter your body dimensions.
    • Set your personal preferences.
    • The rest is calculated using the prior riders' algorithms.
    • Your board will be re-engineered instantly.
    • Remember to upload your graphics.
  • Julian clicks the start button.
  • The modal closes to reveal a 3d snowboard, a body and board specification interface and the snowboarder who the design is based on.
  • Julian enters his height, weight and foot size.
  • The website resized the 3d model based on the body dimension changes.
  • Julian then sets his personal preferences for ability level, stance and board length.
  • The website resized the 3d model based on the personal preference changes.
  • Julian didn't know the snowboard that the design was based on, so he scrolled through the riders on the site until he found someone who's riding he respected.
  • The website resized the 3d model based on the new rider's design algorithm.
  • Julian then scrolled through the recommended board specifications to confirm a good design.
  • Julian also rotates the 3d model around to confirm that he likes the board design
  • Liking the shape and specs of the board, Julian then clicks the "Upload Graphic" button.
  • The website opens a modal with a browse for file button.
  • Julian clicks the browse button and selects an image from his iphone.
  • The image is uploaded and applied to the top of the 3d model.
  • Julian likes look of the top graphic and clicks the "add to cart" button.
